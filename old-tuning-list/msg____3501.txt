3. Arsenic and old lace, Jukka Tiensuu. Voor microtonaal clavecimbel en

More news for Tiensuu fans: "Arsenic and old lace" is now available on 
CD, performed by the Arditti Quartet and himself. Other works on this CD
"From Scandinavia" by Magnus Lindberg, Bent So/rensen and Kaija Saariaho.
Auvidis Montaigne MO 782033.

A work by the Dutch composer Ton Bruyne`l for piano and microtonal tape,
"Brouillard", is on the new CD "Looking Ears III" with music by Bruyne`l.
Donemus CD near 01.
He is also working on a new piece for string quartet and microtonal tape 
commisioned by the Keizer Kwartet.

Roderik de Man has paraphrased Helmholtz in the title of a vocal work,
"On Sensations of Tone". I don't know if it's microtonal but de Man has
written microtonal works.
