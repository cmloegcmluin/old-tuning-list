From: csz@wco.com (Carter Scholz)

>Can individual notes on individual channels be tuned in real-time via
>Sys-ex  on the SY-77/-99 and TG-77 ?

Not on individual channels.  Sys-ex by definition is not channelized.
A single-note tuning change message will change the frequency value for
that one note in the tuning table, which will affect that note on all
channels.
