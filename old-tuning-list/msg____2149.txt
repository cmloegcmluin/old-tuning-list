Subject: Announcement of concert

From: COUL@ezh.nl (Manuel Op de Coul)

On Sunday 29 October there is a concert with lecture about the tuning
of the harp in different centuries. Music from various periods will be
played on an appropriately tuned harp. 
Riet Keppel plays the harp and Jaap Keppel gives the lecture. It will
take place in the Teylers Museum, Spaarne 16, Haarlem and begins at
15.00 h.
