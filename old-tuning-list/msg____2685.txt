Subject: Announcement of radio programme

From: COUL@ezh.nl (Manuel Op de Coul)

Next Monday 25-3 is on Dutch Radio 4 from 20.02 - 24.00 the programme
Supplement from NPS/VPRO devoted to La Monte Young.
New York, August 1995, Co de Kloet and Michael Fahres are at the
doorstep of La Monte Young's house and have a 4 h. interview with him.
The programme has further old work and new recordings. His "Dream
House", a sound sculpture that was in the old stock exchange of New
York has been rebuilt inside his new house and is open for the public.
