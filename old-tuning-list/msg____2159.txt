Subject: John Cage-day

From: COUL@ezh.nl (Manuel Op de Coul)

On Saturday 11 november 12:00 - 24:00 h. there is a John Cage-day in the

Nederlands Filmmuseum
Vondelpark 3 
Amsterdam

"House full of Music"
een Cage-dag in co-produktie met Frank Scheffer, het Nederlands
Filmmuseum, Allegri Film, Andrew Culver, WDR Studio Akustische
Kunst (curator: Klaus Schoning), Stedelijk Museum en AVRO-radio.
with:
Frances-Marie Uitti - cello
Gene Carl - piano
and members of the Ives ensemble.
Also with films by Frank Scheffer with and about John Cage.
The programme includes some microtonal works.

More information:
http://www.netcetera.nl/E41.html
