From: neil.haverstick@csst.com (Neil Haverstick)

Haverstick here...the first annual MICROSTOCK festival happens October
21st, 1995, in Denver, Colorado, at Cameron church, 1600 S. Pearl street
at 8:00 PM. Featured performers include: JOHN SCHNEIDER, on Just
Intonated classical guitar; JOHNNY REINHARD, bassoon, and members of the
American Festival of Microtonal Music, NYC (Mayumi Reinhard, kbds; David
Eggar, cello; Guy Tyler, bass); and the NEIL HAVERSTICK band (Hstick, 19
and 34 tone guitar; John Starrett, 19/34 bass; Ernie Crews,
percussion).Tickets are a mere 10 bucks, and we will try our best to put
up any daring microtonalists who wish to come to Denver for the show.
Should be killer, and we're getting good press already. See you
there,HHHHHHH
